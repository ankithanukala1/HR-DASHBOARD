# HR-DASHBOARD

#DISCRIPTION
Designed an interactive HR Dashboard to track employee headcount, attrition, recruitment trends, and demographics, enabling data-driven HR decisions and improved workforce planning.

#TECHNOLOGIES
Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development & visualization
Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation (ETL)
DAX – Calculated measures & KPIs (attrition rate, headcount)
Data Modeling – Relationships between HR tables
Excel – Data source & extraction
Power BI Service – Publishing, sharing & scheduled refresh
Slicers & Filters – Interactive analysis

#Features of HR Dashboard
Displays total employee count (1.47K) with gender-wise distribution (Male & Female)
Department-wise employee count by job roles
Service year analysis showing employee tenure distribution
Tracks employees on service and overall performance score
Identifies employees due for promotion and not due
Job level–wise employee distribution
Employee status analysis (Very Close, Close, So Far)
Interactive  charts, and visuals for quick HR insights
Supports data-driven workforce planning and decision-making
